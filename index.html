About Me

I'm Binyam, a passionate Video Editor, Motion Designer, and AI Video Specialist based in Addis Ababa. With over 8 years of experience, I've had the pleasure of creating engaging content that has reached a massive audience, including amassing over 590K+ TikTok followers and 10K+ YouTube subscribers for my channel, ETHIOSTAR TIMES.

My unique approach combines traditional editing skills using industry-standard software like Adobe Premiere, After Effects, and Blender, with the power of cutting-edge AI tools such as Runway, Sora, Pika, and HeyGen. This fusion allows me to produce high-quality, on-trend videos quickly and efficiently.

I'm dedicated to bringing your vision to life with a professional polish that captures attention and delivers measurable results.
